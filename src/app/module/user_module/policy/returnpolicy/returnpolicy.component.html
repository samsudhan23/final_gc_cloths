Return Policy

Easy and transparent return process at CLONO.

CLONO offers a hassle-free return process to ensure customer satisfaction. We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied, returns are accepted under the following conditions.

Return Eligibility

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Invoice or proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

Discounted items, clearance sales, and hygiene-sensitive products cannot be returned.

Return Conditions

Items must be unused, unwashed, and returned in original packaging with tags intact.
